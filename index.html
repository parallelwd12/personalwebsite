About Me

Name: Yazhi Xing
Degree: MD/PhD
Profession: Doctor

I am a medical doctor and researcher specializing in hearing and vestibular research. My clinical work focuses on hearing loss, tinnitus, and vertigo. I am affiliated with Shanghai Jiao Tong University's Sixth People's Hospital, where I lead various research projects aimed at understanding and treating auditory disorders. My contributions to the field include numerous publications on the pathology and treatment of auditory nerve conditions and vestibular disorders.
